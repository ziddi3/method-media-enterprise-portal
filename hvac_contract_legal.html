1. Project Overview

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Method Media ("Service Provider") and Arpi's Industries Ltd ("Client") for the development and implementation of a custom HVAC Operational System. The System is designed to increase technician efficiency, quote accuracy, and brand authority through operational leverage.

The Client agrees to engage the Service Provider to develop a fully customized operational system under the Client's company name, branding, workflows, and internal processes.

2. Scope of Services

The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services:

  • Custom system design and architecture
  • Client logo and company name integration
  • Custom workflow configuration
  • Branded technician interface development
  • Custom pricing logic and templates
  • Optional CRM integrations
  • Dedicated deployment and implementation
  • 30 days of post-launch support

3. Payment Structure - Milestone Model

The total project investment is $59,000 CAD, payable in five (5) sequential milestones. Each milestone must be reviewed, approved, signed off, and paid in full before the next phase begins.

Milestone Description Amount
1 Discovery & System Mapping $8,500
2 Core Build & Workflow Engine $14,000
3 Custom Branding & UI Layer $12,500
4 Integration & Testing $12,500
5 Deployment + Training + Support $11,500
TOTAL PROJECT INVESTMENT $59,000

4. Milestone Terms

Each milestone includes the following conditions:

  • Service Provider completes milestone deliverables
  • Client reviews and tests deliverables
  • Client provides written approval or change requests
  • Client pays milestone invoice in full
  • Work on next milestone commences only after payment

Payment Terms: All invoices are due within 14 days of invoice date. Late payments may result in project delays.

5. Intellectual Property

Upon final payment of Milestone 5, the Service Provider transfers 100% ownership of the custom code, database schema, and system architecture to the Client. The Service Provider retains rights to its underlying reusable code libraries and frameworks.

6. Project Timeline

The estimated project completion time is 12-16 weeks from the start date, contingent upon timely Client feedback and approval at each milestone. Delays in Client approval may extend the timeline.

Important Terms & Conditions

  • Termination: The Client may terminate this Agreement at the end of any completed milestone by not funding the next milestone. No refunds are issued for completed or in-progress milestones.
  • Support: 30 days of post-launch support included. Extended support contracts are available upon request.
  • Changes: Additional features beyond the agreed scope will be scoped as separate milestones with separate pricing.
  • Warranty: The Service Provider warrants that the System will function as specified for 90 days post-launch, excluding bugs caused by Client modifications or third-party integrations.
  • Confidentiality: Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during this project.

7. Acceptance

By signing below, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The Client accepts the milestone payment structure and agrees to the total project investment of $59,000 CAD.
